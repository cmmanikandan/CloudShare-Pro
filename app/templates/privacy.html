Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

CloudShare Pro collects minimal data necessary to provide our services. This includes your email address for account management and metadata for files you upload (filename, size, and expiration settings).

2. File Security & Encryption

All files uploaded to CloudShare Pro are stored using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We do not have access to the contents of your files unless you explicitly share them via public links.

For P2P transfers, data is sent directly between devices and never touches our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudinary for secure file storage and Flask-Mail for email notifications. These providers are strictly vetted for security and compliance with international data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA).

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. Deleting a file from your dashboard removes all traces from our servers and storage providers immediately.

5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@cloudshare.pro.
